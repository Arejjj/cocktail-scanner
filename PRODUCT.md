# Product Definition

## Problem

Home mixology enthusiasts struggle to build a collection that truly reflects the drinks they love because great cocktails are discovered in fleeting moments — at bars, events, or travels — and the details fade fast. Menus offer only names and vague ingredients, and online lookups return generic versions far from what they actually tasted. The result is a personal library that stays forever incomplete, never quite capturing the experiences that sparked it.

## Target Audience

Home bartenders who dine out or travel regularly and want to build a personal recipe collection from the cocktails they discover in the wild.

## Solution

I will build a mobile-first scanning tool that helps home bartenders who discover cocktails when they're out turn fleeting menu encounters into a permanent, personal recipe collection — by capturing the drink in the moment, before the details fade.

## Concept Summary

When home bartenders discover a great cocktail out in the world, the details — ingredients, proportions, what made it special — are gone before they ever make it home.

**For:** home bartenders who drink out and want to build a personal recipe collection.

Mixology AI is a mobile scanning tool that lets you photograph a bar menu on the spot and walk away with a complete, saved recipe. It captures the drink in the moment — when the memory is fresh and the menu is still in front of you — so your collection actually reflects the drinks you've loved, not just the ones you remembered to look up later.
